# Service Desk Documentation

Welcome to the Service Desk documentation repository. This repository contains comprehensive documentation for the Service Desk organization, including service definitions, work culture framework, and strategic roadmap.

## Overview

The Service Desk serves as the single point of contact (SPOC) for IT support across all global locations, supporting our automotive parts production business with critical IT services and ensuring business continuity.

### Key Characteristics
- **Global Coverage:** Service Desk team operates across multiple global locations
- **24/7 Support:** Round-the-clock support for critical business operations
- **Industry Focus:** Specialized support for automotive manufacturing environment
- **Business Critical:** Essential support function for IT and business continuity
