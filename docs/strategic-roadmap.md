# Service Desk - Strategic Roadmap

## Executive Summary

This roadmap outlines the strategic transformation of the Service Desk from its current state to a world-class support organization aligned with our automotive manufacturing business needs. The roadmap is structured in four phases over 18 months, focusing on service excellence, cultural transformation, and operational efficiency.

## Current State Assessment

### Strengths
- 24/7 coverage across global locations
- Dedicated team supporting critical business operations
- Established presence in key manufacturing sites
- Multi-location support capability

### Challenges
- Inconsistent service delivery across locations
- Limited knowledge sharing between sites
- Varying technical skill levels
- Lack of standardized processes
- Need for stronger alignment with organizational values
- Limited self-service capabilities
- Manual, time-consuming processes

### Gap Analysis
| Area | Current State | Target State | Gap |
|------|--------------|--------------|-----|
| Service Standardization | Location-specific processes | Global standardized processes | High |
| Knowledge Management | Siloed information | Centralized knowledge base | High |
| Automation | Manual ticket handling | Automated workflows & self-service | Medium |
| Skills & Training | Ad-hoc training | Structured development programs | Medium |
| Culture Alignment | Informal culture | Values-driven culture framework | High |
| Metrics & Reporting | Basic metrics | Comprehensive analytics | Medium |
| User Experience | Reactive support | Proactive, user-friendly service | High |

## Target State Vision

### Service Excellence
- Consistent, high-quality support across all locations
- First-contact resolution rate > 75%
- Customer satisfaction score > 90%
- Average resolution time reduced by 40%
- 24/7 seamless follow-the-sun support

### Cultural Transformation
- Unified global team culture aligned with organizational values
- High employee engagement and satisfaction
- Knowledge-sharing culture across locations
- Continuous learning and development mindset
- Recognition and reward programs

### Operational Efficiency
- Automated routine tasks and requests
- Comprehensive self-service portal
- Integrated ITSM tools across locations
- Data-driven decision making
- Optimized resource allocation

## Roadmap Phases

## Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-4)

### Objectives
- Establish baseline metrics and standards
- Define and communicate service catalog
- Launch cultural transformation initiatives
- Standardize core processes

### Key Initiatives

#### 1.1 Service Catalog Development
- **Timeline:** Month 1-2
- **Deliverables:**
  - Complete service catalog documentation
  - Define SLAs for all services
  - Publish user-facing service catalog
- **Success Criteria:** Service catalog published and communicated to all users

#### 1.2 Process Standardization
- **Timeline:** Month 2-4
- **Deliverables:**
  - Document standard operating procedures (SOPs)
  - Create global process framework
  - Implement common ticketing workflows
- **Success Criteria:** 80% of common processes standardized across locations

#### 1.3 Cultural Framework Launch
- **Timeline:** Month 1-4
- **Deliverables:**
  - Define and publish core values and culture framework
  - Launch team communication plan
  - Initiate regular global team meetings
- **Success Criteria:** 100% team awareness of culture framework

#### 1.4 Baseline Metrics Establishment
- **Timeline:** Month 1-2
- **Deliverables:**
  - Define key performance indicators (KPIs)
  - Implement metrics tracking dashboard
  - Establish baseline measurements
- **Success Criteria:** All KPIs defined and baseline established

### Phase 1 Milestones
- ✓ Service catalog published
- ✓ Core SOPs documented
- ✓ Culture framework launched
- ✓ Metrics dashboard operational

## Risk Management

### Key Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| Resistance to change | High | Medium | Strong change management, clear communication, early wins |
| Resource constraints | High | Medium | Phased approach, prioritization, business case for investments |
