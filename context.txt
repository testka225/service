chapter: Targets
1. Flexibility & Role Distribution

More flexibility in responsibilities (80/20 principle – no rigid 100%)
ONE IT approach (no strict global/local separation)
Better balance across areas (mutual support, shared responsibilities)
Avoid always assigning the same people to projects


2. Resource & Workload Management

Distribute workload more evenly
Ensure sufficient staffing (critical issue from employee survey)
Use existing employees more cross-functionally and promote their skills


3. Clear Expectations & Communication

Define expectations clearly (what we do and what we don’t)
Improve internal communication (clear and consistent across all levels)
Reduce misunderstandings and sensitivities (e.g., license questions, Exchange Online communication)
Address feedback like:

“Why wasn’t I informed?”
“Communication could have been better.”
“I don’t officially know about this.”
“Who decided this and why?”




4. Employee Satisfaction & Collaboration

Increase employee satisfaction (based on survey results)
Strengthen collaboration, especially cross-functional
Foster teamwork and reduce silos
